About the Artist

Kyrie shares a charming home in Logan, Utah with her husband and a cat called Fizz. When Kyrie is not toiling away at her craft, she can be found in her garden, watching television with the cat, or repainting the upstairs bathroom, which changes color at least semi-annually. Outside of her home, she can be found sheering sheep, paddleboarding, and wandering the aisles of TJ Max in search of the newest pillow to join her ever-growing collection.

Kyrie enjoys just about every form of artistic expression, but is particularly fond of printmaking, illustration, and needle felting. This site is specifically geared towards her services as an illustrator. Kyrie currently exclusively uses watercolor for her illustrations. If you are looking for a different medium, please feel free to reach out and inquire.
